NAI Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how NAI handles information in connection with the NAI Google Meet add-on only.

1. Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact lironktf@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We designed the Google Meet add-on to require as little information as possible from end users.

  • We may receive basic Google Meet session information needed for the add-on to run, such as a meeting identifier and participant status.
  • We may process limited technical information, such as timestamps and service logs, to keep the add-on available, secure, and reliable.
  • If you contact us directly, we will receive the information you include in that message.

We do not require end users of the Google Meet add-on to create a separate NAI account just to use the add-on.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • operate the Google Meet add-on;
  • maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug issues;
  • respond to support requests or legal obligations; and
  • improve stability and functionality of the add-on.

4. Legal Bases

Where required by law, we process personal information based on one or more of the following: your consent, performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests in securing and operating the Service.

5. Sharing of Information

We may share information with:

  • service providers that help us host, secure, and operate the add-on;
  • Google, to the extent necessary for the Google Meet add-on to work within Google's platform;
  • legal or regulatory authorities when required by law; and
  • a successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, or similar business transaction.

6. Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the add-on, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

7. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect personal information. No security measure is perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability of certain personal information, and to withdraw consent where consent is the basis for processing.

9. Children

The add-on is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Transfers

Personal information may be processed in countries other than the country where you are located. Where required, we take steps intended to provide appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Third-Party Services

The add-on runs within Google Meet and may rely on third-party hosting or infrastructure providers. Those third parties govern their own privacy practices under their own policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above when changes are made.
