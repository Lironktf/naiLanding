NAI Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how NAI collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information in connection with the NAI identity verification platform, including the web app, mobile app, Google Meet add-on, and related integrations.

1. Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact lironktf@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • account information, such as name, email address, phone number, and account credentials;
  • identity verification information, including government ID verification results and related metadata from KYC providers;
  • biometric and liveness-related information, such as selfies, face comparison scores, liveness session IDs, and verification outcomes;
  • integration data, such as Google Meet codes, Telegram IDs, Telegram usernames, Discord IDs, Discord usernames, and session participation status;
  • technical information, such as IP address, browser type, device information, logs, and timestamps; and
  • support or communications data when you contact us.

3. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

  • provide and secure the Service;
  • create and manage user accounts;
  • perform identity verification and liveness checks;
  • link verification results to supported integrations;
  • detect fraud, abuse, and security incidents;
  • improve reliability, performance, and product functionality;
  • comply with legal obligations; and
  • respond to user inquiries and support requests.

4. Legal Bases

Where required by law, we process personal information based on one or more of the following: your consent, performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests in securing and operating the Service.

5. Sharing of Information

We may share information with:

  • service providers that help us host, secure, analyze, and operate the Service;
  • identity, liveness, cloud, or communications providers involved in delivering verification functionality;
  • integration platforms such as Google, Telegram, or Discord, when necessary to support the requested workflow;
  • legal or regulatory authorities when required by law or to protect rights, safety, or the Service; and
  • a successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, or similar business transaction.

6. Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain security and audit records. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the workflow involved.

7. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect personal information. No security measure is perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability of certain personal information, and to withdraw consent where consent is the basis for processing.

9. Children

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Transfers

Personal information may be processed in countries other than the country where you are located. Where required, we take steps intended to provide appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Third-Party Services

The Service may link to or rely on third-party platforms and service providers. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above when changes are made.
